ARTIST BIOGRAPHY

Nina Smith currently attends Juniata College where she studies Integrated Media Arts with a secondary emphasis in Communication. Her work focuses on digital media, including digital art and photography, where she explores visual storytelling through design. Originally from Fairfield, Pennsylvania, Nina has a strong creative background shaped by both visual arts and theater, having participated in several stage productions throughout her life. After graduation, she hopes to pursue a career in graphic or brand design.
